CTE Awareness & Connections

Understanding the broader impact on families, communities, and the sports world

Community Impact

Tony Dorsett’s CTE has deeply affected his family and daily life after football. He experiences memory loss, mood changes, and confusion, sometimes forgetting where he is going or how to get to his daughters’ practices. His children were often afraid because they never knew what version of him they would see each day.

Tony’s condition shows how CTE impacts entire families, not just athletes.

Legal & Professional Awareness

Tony Dorsett played twelve seasons in the NFL and suffered more than three diagnosed concussions. Despite winning the Heisman Trophy, a Super Bowl, and being inducted into the Hall of Fame, he now struggles with light sensitivity, memory loss, and speech difficulties. He has even questioned who he is becoming as the disease continues to affect his life.

Tony’s story highlights the long-term consequences of repeated head injuries.
