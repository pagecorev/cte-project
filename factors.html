Causes & Risk Factors

What contributes to CTE in athletes and others

Repeated Head Trauma

Multiple concussions or sub-concussive impacts are the primary risk factors for CTE. Sports like football, hockey, and boxing are especially high risk, due to the agression and level of impacts in the sports.

Repeated impacts in contact sports increase long-term risk.

Genetics & Other Factors

Some individuals may have higher susceptibility to brain degeneration due to genetics or prior neurological conditions. Age, sport intensity, and protective equipment also play roles.

Brain imaging studies reveal structural changes linked to repeated trauma.
