Support systems, therapy, and prevention strategies for CTE

Therapy & Mental Health Support

Professional therapy and counseling can help manage emotional symptoms. It is shown that early intervention of CTE (if predicted) can lead to much better results-the earlier that one seeks help, the faster they can recover.

Therapy provides crucial support for affected individuals.

Awareness

It is important to make sure that families, players, and coaches are aware of CTE, as not only can they help prevent it, but they will also know the proper actions to take when hard collisions inevitably occur.

Prevention & Safety Measures

Proper equipment, rule changes, and safe practices in sports can reduce CTE risk. Education and awareness for athletes, parents, and coaches is critical.

Safety measures help reduce the likelihood of brain injury.
